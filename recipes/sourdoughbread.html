Sourdough bread

Make bread using sourdough starter

There are many ways to make bread and this recipe comes from the blog: alimento y conciencia.

This recipe uses sourdough starter. If you don't have one, please visit this page to learn how to make it.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 tbsp. salt
  • 2 o 3 tbsp. olive oil optional
  • 1 a 1 ¼ cups of water approx.

Steps

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl. Mix well.
  2. Make a hole in the middle of the mix and add the sourdough starer and the olive oil.
  3. Begin pouring the water while kneading. The water measurement should adjust to you particular conditions.
  4. Knead the dough for about 10 min. The dough should be tender. Add more water if needed.
  5. Cover the bowl with a wet cloth and leave to rise at room temperature (ideally between 20 and 28 ° C) from 8 to 16 hs.
  6. Knead the dough again for about 5 min. on a floured surface
  7. Roll the dough and mold it.
  8. Place it in the mold, previously oiled.
  9. Let it rise at room temperature, from 1 to 4 hours. (until it expands and grows at least 50% in size.)
  10. Bake at medium temperature 180 – 200 °C, for 60 min. approx.
  11. Unmold and cool it on a rack or wood, so it can give off moisture.
